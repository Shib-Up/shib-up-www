The first automatic rebasing token to provide $SHIB rewards to the holders. Chart programmed to stay in a constant climb, what are you waiting for? ShibUp is a price-elastic token with an adjusting supply. Rebates make price-elastic tokens into synthetic commodities with fluctuating values and supplies that gradually stabilize. Ultimately, rebates are designed to be tradable and potentially extremely profitable. And now with Shiba Inu Token rewards!

Rebase explained.

ShibUp is an elastic supply token, which means that the total supply of the token is changing. Supply and price changes happen every now and then and they are called “Rebates”. Rebates will push the price to a rising price peg by burning supply.

It looks something likes this:
Rebase is called
->Overall tokens in the ecosystem decreases (including tokens in your wallet)
->The price of the token is bumped up
->Overall value of your holdings remain the same.

The decrease in tokens is offset by the increase in price and the total value of your holding is unchanged. You will still own the same % of the total token supply as before the rebase.

It is important to note that rebases DO NOT affect the actual value of your holdings.

How does it help?

Rebasing mechanism guarantees that the price of the token will always go up. This will keep the chart on listing sites green as the charts follow the token price.
Constantly rising chart helps us get trending on all the largest listing sites such as CoinGecko, Dextools, CoinmarketCap, thus bringing new investors to the ShibUp family.

Rising chart combined with $SHIB rewards as an incentive to hold will help investors earn returns never seen on the market before.

Tokenomics of ShibUp

ShibUp is the first Rebase token to bring Shiba Inu ($SHIB) rewards as an additional incentive to hold and earn.

6% Marketing & Buyback

5% SHIB Rewards
